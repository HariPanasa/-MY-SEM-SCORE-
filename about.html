About & Help

Everything you need to know about calculating your academic scores.

📚 What is SGPA?

Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) is a measure of performance of work done in a semester. It is ratio of total credit points secured by a student in various courses registered in a semester and the total course credits taken during that semester.

📈 What is CGPA?

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is a measure of overall cumulative performance of a student over all semesters. It is calculated by taking the average of all SGPAs across your completed semesters.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my Percentage from CGPA?
We use the standard university formula to calculate percentage: Percentage = (CGPA - 0.75) × 10. You can use our dedicated Percentage Converter to do this instantly.
Are my results saved permanently?
No. This platform is completely private. Any results you calculate are processed instantly on your device and are never sent to a server.
How do I copy my results?
Once you calculate your SGPA, CGPA, or Percentage, a "Copy Result" button will appear. Clicking it will automatically format your results and copy them to your clipboard so you can easily paste them in a message or document.
